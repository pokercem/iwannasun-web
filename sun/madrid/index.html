About iwannasun

Why cloud percentage can mislead: Cloud percentage does not tell you if the sun is actually blocked, especially when the sun is low on the horizon.

What iwannasun does differently: iwannasun follows the sun's exact path and checks the clouds along that path, so you know when sunlight really breaks through.

Sun Score: The chance that the sun is directly visible.
Confidence: How steady that situation is likely to stay.

Tip: Add this page to your home screen for a fast daily check.
