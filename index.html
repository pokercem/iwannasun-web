About
This app tells you if the sun will actually reach you.

Not whether it’s warm.
Just whether the sun itself is blocked or not.

Sun Score is how likely you’ll be in direct sunlight.
High = you’ll feel the sun.
Low = clouds will stop it.

Confidence is how steady that prediction is.
Broken clouds and low sun make things uncertain.

When the sun is low, far-away clouds can hide it.
When it’s high, only nearby clouds matter.

Made by sun enthusiasts, for sun enthusiasts ☀️
