About
Sunlight is a simple wellness lever: it can lift mood, help you feel awake, and make a short walk feel like a reset.

The tricky part is that “cloudy” doesn’t always mean “no sun.” What matters is whether there’s a clear line between you and the sun — especially near sunrise/sunset when the sun is low and distant clouds matter most.

This app follows the sun’s path and checks the clouds along that path, so you can plan a quick sun break when it counts.

Sun Score = how likely the sun is directly visible.
Confidence = how steady that situation is likely to stay.
Tip: Add this page to your home screen for a fast daily check.
